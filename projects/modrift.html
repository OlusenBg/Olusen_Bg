What modrift actually does

If you've ever modded a game with a big load order, you know the frustration: mods conflict with each other in ways that are hard to diagnose. Something breaks, you don't know what, and tracking it down takes longer than playing the game.

modrift is an AI-powered conflict detection and CI/CD platform for mod developers. It scans your mod list, identifies conflicts, and gives you one-click tools to ship to Nexus Mods. The AI assistant (Claude API) helps understand what's actually conflicting and why. Cloudflare Workers runs the backend, Neon handles the data, Clerk handles auth.

Still in early access. The core conflict detection works — the rest is being built around it.

Key features

AI conflict detection

Scans your mod load order and uses Claude to identify conflicts, explain what's clashing, and suggest fixes. Not just a red flag — an actual diagnosis.

One-click CI/CD

Ship directly to Nexus Mods in one click. Automated pipeline handles packaging, versioning, and upload so you can focus on the mod itself.

Cross-game support

Skyrim, Minecraft, and others. One platform, same workflow regardless of which game you're modding.

Cloudflare-powered backend

Cloudflare Workers at the edge, Neon DB for persistence, Clerk for auth. Fast and solid infrastructure for a platform still in early access.
